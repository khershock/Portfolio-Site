Introduction

My name is Kory Hershock and I am a Web Developer out of Marshall, MI. I obtained a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science from Olivet College. Aside from creating websites and other software, I love participating in and attending sporting events. I am currently taking projects, so if you would like to inquire about hiring me or just want to send me a message, go ahead and do so. I look forward to hearing from you.

My Story

I was born in Detroit, MI and moved to Marshall, MI at a very young age. After High School I wasn't sure what I wanted to do with the rest of my life, so I took a couple years off college and took over the family business, Hungry Howies Pizza. After realizing that I wanted to work with computers, I went back to college and finished within a year of being back. I love the way a beutifully designed website looks and functions, and I hope to be able to provide that service and more to great people.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why did I choose to go into Web Development?

The thing I love most about web development is the fact that I can work from anywhere in the world. I love the process in which it takes to build a professional website, it keeps me on my toes not knowing what type of project I will have next. I chose web developent to hopefully someday live my dream life of traveling the world while making an honest living remotely.

Which web developer inspires me the most?

When I first started to learn about web development, I was taking online classes through Brad Hussey, my favorite web developer. The reason he inspires me so much is because he started just like any of us would, from the ground up. He is a freelance web developer who makes a living through his online businesses and classes. Be sure to check him out!
